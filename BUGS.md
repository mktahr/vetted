- **[signals] Athletics extractor false-positives on context-free "olympian"/"olympic" substring match.** Surfaced 2026-06-30 (gated-promotion real-data test). A promoted Notion connection (Annie Cheng) was tagged `Olympic Athlete` because the `pattern_extractor` matched the alias **"olympian"** inside an Airbnb *role description* — "Online **Olympian** & Paralympian festivals hosted in partnership with the IOC" (a product launch she supported, not an athletic credential). `extractPatterns` does context-free substring matching against `signal_dictionary.aliases`, so any text containing "olympian"/"olympic" → "Olympic Athlete". Fix options: (a) require athletics signals to come from `activities_honors`/education-activities fields, not experience `description_raw`; (b) tighten the aliases / add negative context (e.g. exclude when adjacent to "festival", "campaign", "partnership", "sponsor"); (c) gate athletics behind a higher confidence / manual-verify. Same class of risk for other broad single-word aliases. (Annie's bad signal row was manually deleted during the test; it will re-fire on re-extract until the extractor is fixed.) ~0.5 day.
