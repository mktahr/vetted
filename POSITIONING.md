# Vetted — Positioning

## Header

Talent and company discovery, ranked by signal.

## Sub-header

Vetted is the only recruiting platform that finds the right companies and the right people inside them — scored on talent density and trajectory, not keywords and brand-name filters.

## Signal Grid (Section 2)

1. Hackathons
2. Fellowships
3. Founder Experience
4. Elite Collegiate Teams
5. Open Source Contributions
6. Research Labs & Publications (coming soon)

## Positioning Notes

- Category: candidate AND company discovery in one platform
- Differentiator vs Harmonic: recruiting-first, not VC-first
- Differentiator vs Juicebox: company discovery layer, not candidate-only
- Differentiator vs LinkedIn: signal-based scoring, no UGC noise
- Core signals scored: trajectory, tenure, talent density, time-in-company context
- Surface signals (the grid): things no one else searches on
